Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task


						Changing the laptop battery:
							1.	Look for the right battery model and order it
							2.	Unscrew the cover on the bottom of the laptop
							3.	Remove bolts around the battery
							4.	Take the original battery out
							5.	Put in the new battery
							6.	Screw the bolts back in to secure the battery
							7.	Put back the cover on the back for the laptop
							8.	Turn on the laptop and charge the battery to validate if the new battery works.
